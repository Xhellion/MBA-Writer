## MBA 关键词库

推荐关键词按 MBA 研究方向分类：
- **战略管理**: competitive strategy, strategic positioning, industry analysis
- **组织行为**: organizational culture, leadership, employee engagement
- **市场营销**: customer loyalty, brand equity, market orientation
- **人力资源**: talent management, performance appraisal, incentive mechanism
- **运营管理**: supply chain, quality management, process optimization
- **创新管理**: innovation capability, technology adoption, R&D strategy
- **财务管理**: financial performance, capital structure, investment efficiency
